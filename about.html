Tritone Music Producers conference 2023

The conference is an international, cosmopolitan event that birngs together all manner of artists, tech lovers, music lovers, but mostly music producers, music theorists, and musicians every year.
This year, we hope to bring you an experience that will blow your minds (in the best way), and elevate your appreciation of music and the music production process.
We invite you to join us if you find yourself within the bracket of different types of individuals mentioned above, or if your imagination has been tickled by the stories or just by visiting this site! Please contact us via Email for any further questions about TMC production conference Tritonemusic.co@enquiries.org
